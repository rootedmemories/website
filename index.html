What you’ll be able to do

Build your family tree, drop in photos, captions, and stories. All connected to the right person so it stays organised as the years go by. Keep all your children's photos structured cleanly, and eventually hand over their profile to take over.

1) Build the tree

Add relatives once. Invite family so they can contribute safely, without the mess of endless group chats.

2) Add memories to people

Photos + stories actually make sense when they’re attached to the the right person (with dates and context). View photos by person, or in the full family feed.

3) Keep it for life

A living archive your kids can open in 10–20 years and still feel like they know everyone.
