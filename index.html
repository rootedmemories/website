Welcome to Rooted Memories

A private family archive to store photos and stories, organised by your family tree, so nothing gets lost when phones break, people pass, or time moves on.

Most families don’t lose memories in one big moment. They lose them in a thousand tiny ones. A phone upgrade. A lost password. A chat that disappears. A relative who’s always meaning to write it down. Rooted Memories turns family history into something that actually survives.

You are not just recording the past. You are building yours and your children’s lives in real time.
Built for parents, grandparents, and anyone who wants a private family filing cabinet that lasts.
