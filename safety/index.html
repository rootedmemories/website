ROOTED MEMORIES – CHILD SAFETY STANDARDS

1. Our Commitment

Rooted Memories is committed to preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We do not tolerate any form of abusive, exploitative, or illegal content.

2. Prohibited Content

Users are strictly prohibited from uploading, sharing, or storing any content that involves:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Sexual exploitation of minors
  • Any illegal or harmful content involving children

3. Reporting

Users can report any concerning content by contacting us directly. Reports are reviewed and acted upon promptly.

support@rootedmemories.co.uk

4. Enforcement

We reserve the right to remove content, suspend accounts, and report illegal activity to relevant authorities where required.

5. Cooperation with Authorities

Rooted Memories cooperates with law enforcement and regulatory authorities in cases involving illegal content.
